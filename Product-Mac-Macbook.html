Product Information

The new MacBook Pro M1 16 Core-GPU (16-inch) delivers impressive high performance for pro users. Choose from the powerful M1 Pro or the even more powerful M1 Max to accelerate pro-level workflows with incredible battery life. With a vibrant 16-inch Liquid Retina XDR display and multiple professional ports, you can do more than ever with MacBook Pro.

Remarkable features
  • The Apple-designed M1 Max or M1 Pro chip creates a quantum leap in machine learning, CPU, and GPU performance.
  • Up to 10-core CPU for up to 2x faster performance, handling professional workflows faster.
  • Up to 32-core GPU with up to 4x faster graphics processing speed for graphics-intensive applications and games.
  • 16-core Neural Engine for up to 5x faster machine learning performance.
  • Longer battery life, up to 21 hours.
  • Unified memory up to 64GB helps you do everything quickly and smoothly.
  • Super fast SSD storage up to 8TB helps open applications and files in a snap.
  • Sharp 16-inch Liquid Retina XDR display with Extreme Dynamic Range and extremely high contrast ratio.
  • 1080p FaceTime HD camera with advanced image signal processor for sharper video calls.
  • 6-speaker sound system with power-cancelling subwoofer.
  • Three studio-quality microphones capture your voice more clearly.
  • New generation Wi-Fi 6 helps connect faster.
  • The backlit Magic Keyboard and Touch ID make unlocking and paying more secure.
  • macOS Monterey lets you connect, share, and create like never before, with FaceTime updates exciting new and redesigned Safari.
